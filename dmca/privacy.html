Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to understand how our service is used. This includes general information such as country-level location and anonymous usage data like pages visited and time spent on the site. Exact IP addresses and personal identifying data are not collected.

How We Use Information

Collected data helps improve performance, monitor site stability, and analyze general usage trends. All information is reviewed in aggregate and is not used to identify individual users.

Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be removed promptly.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics, which operates under Google’s own privacy policies. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to collect anonymous interaction data. This data does not include exact IP addresses.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are in place to protect collected data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised update date.
